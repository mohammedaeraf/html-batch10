Steps to prepare Indian Tea

  1. Boil 500 ml water in a tea pot
  2. Add 3 Tsp Sugar and 2 Tsb Tea Powder
  3. Let it boil for 10 mins
  4. Add milk
  5. Pour the tea in cups
